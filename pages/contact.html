Frequently Asked Questions

Is OpenReaders free to use? +

OpenReaders is free for readers to explore stories. Some books offer free samples (first 2 chapters), and full access requires a one-time payment.

What types of stories do you have? +

We feature diverse stories including personal incidents, novels, memoirs, and real-life narratives. Whether you're looking for stories that inspire, comfort, challenge, or entertain, you'll find something that speaks to you on OpenReaders.

Is my payment information secure? +

Yes! We use Razorpay for all payments, which is PCI-DSS compliant and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

Can I download books to read offline? +

Currently, books are available to read on our platform. We're working on offline reading features for the future.

Can I share stories with my friends? +

Absolutely! Share your favorite stories with friends and family. You can also join our community and connect with other readers who love the same stories you do.
